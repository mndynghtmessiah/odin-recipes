Butter Noodles

Description

An easy, dependable meal. Great for autistic people and/or the overworked, burnt out capitalist cog in your life. You can add anything as you go to your tastes, that's the beauty of it.

Ingredients

  • Noodles of your choosing (I prefer spiral egg noodles)
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese (optional, for topping)

Steps

  1. Set out a pot of water to boil. Make sure it's at least half the pot, in order to cover the noodles entirely. Salt your water.
  2. Once water is boiling, add your noodles and turn the heat down to medium-high.
  3. Once the noodles are al dente, strain.
  4. Back on the burner, melt your butter in your pot. Brown it just barely.
  5. Add your noodles, salt, and pepper, and give it a good mix.
  6. Add parmesan on top, if you'd like.
  7. That's it! Enjoy an easy safe food.
